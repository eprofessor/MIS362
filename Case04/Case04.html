Case Overview

SmartBean Coffee Software Modernization

SmartBean Coffee recently completed a major hardware upgrade across all stores, but their software is still lagging behind.
They use different systems for inventory, payroll, scheduling, and marketing-many of which are outdated or incompatible.
The company wants to move toward integrated, cloud-based solutions that work well together and support future growth.
You have been asked to help management understand the difference between system software and application software, what to consider when selecting new software, and current software trends that could impact their decision-making.
